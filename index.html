Bio

I am a doctoral student in statistics at the University of South Carolina. Previously, I graduated from Rutgers University - New Brunswick with a bachelor's of arts in statistics/mathematics. During my time there, I was a member of Rutgers Chess Club and served as treasurer of the Iota Tau chapter of Pi Kappa Phi during the 2020-2021 term. I also worked as a research assistant for many different groups at Rutgers, including the Center for Alcohol Studies and the Eagleton Center for Public Interest Polling.
